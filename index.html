Biryani is a popular and flavorful South Asian rice dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It's known for its aromatic blend of spices, tender meat, fragrant rice, and sometimes vegetables. Biryani is considered a complete meal in itself and is often enjoyed during special occasions, celebrations, or as a comforting family meal.

Egg gravy, also known as egg curry or Anda masala, is a flavorful and hearty dish made by cooking eggs in a rich and aromatic gravy. This dish is popular in various cuisines, including Indian, Pakistani, and Bangladeshi, and can be enjoyed with rice, roti, naan, or other types of bread.
